Who we serve

A badgeless partner, measured by the science we deliver.

SSAI supports NASA at Goddard, Langley, Ames, Marshall, Stennis, and Headquarters, alongside NOAA, NSF, USDA, EPA, USGS, NIH, the Department of Defense, and partners such as the World Bank. Across these agencies, our scientists, engineers, and analysts have become a fixture of the programs that ask the hardest questions about our planet and the universe.

We work in a badgeless environment alongside our government colleagues and fellow contractors — measured not by which badge we wear, but by the science we deliver. That is the standard we bring to every engagement, and the reason agencies keep us close to their most important missions.

Contract vehicles

Straightforward to work with.

Engage SSAI through GSA Multiple Award Schedule (MAS), OASIS Small Business, SLED contract vehicles, and Other Transaction Authority (OTA) consortia. As a certified woman-owned, SBA-certified small business, we offer prime and subcontract pathways across civilian and defense acquisition.

Whether you need a prime that can carry a complex, high-reliability program or a subcontractor that strengthens your team's past performance and small-business standing, we are positioned to engage on the vehicle that fits your acquisition.

What the certifications let us carry.

Maturity and quality certifications are not decoration — they are the gate to the work. They tell a contracting officer that SSAI can be trusted with high-reliability software, flight-adjacent engineering, and the data systems agencies cannot afford to have fail.

  • High-reliability software — the discipline to build systems that operate missions and archive national science data for decades.
  • Mission-grade engineering — the process rigor that flight programs and their primes expect from every team seat we fill.
  • Auditable delivery — quality management that holds up under government review, on civilian and defense contracts alike.
  • Small-business strength — set-aside eligibility that adds value to prime teams and to agencies' small-business goals.
