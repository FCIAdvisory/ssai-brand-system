Defense & National Security

We engineer the systems that hold when the mission cannot fail.

SSAI delivers cleared, Mil-spec engineering and manufacturing to the defense and national security missions that cannot afford to fail. We carry the rigor proven on NASA's flagship programs to the systems the warfighter depends on, and we deliver it the first time, and every time.

The Mission

We serve the missions that defend the nation.

We support the Department of Defense, the military services, and the defense laboratories on the work that protects the country. We engineer flight hardware, operate in space, defend networks, and manufacture cleared electronics where the standard is mission assurance, not best effort.

Defense and national security missions demand a partner who is already cleared, already certified, and already proven. We hold a Top Secret Facility Clearance and we are ITAR registered. We manufacture to Mil-spec in cleared facilities under two miles from NASA Goddard. We have done this work for more than four decades, and we bring the same discipline to a defense payload that we bring to a flagship science mission.

When a program cannot fail, the question is not whether a system is impressive. The question is whether it performs the first time, and every time. That is the standard we were built to meet.

How We Serve

How we serve Defense & National Security.

We map five capabilities to the defense mission, each one focused on the outcome the warfighter needs: hardware that holds, networks that stay defended, and systems that perform under conditions where there is no second attempt.

01 Engineering

Flight hardware the warfighter can trust

We deliver systems engineering, avionics and software at CMMI Level 3, FPGA design, EEE parts processing, and integration and testing. We engineer to defense standards so the hardware performs in the environments where failure is not an option.

02 Space Operations

Space operations and domain awareness

We develop and calibrate space instruments and operate missions from pre-launch through on-orbit. We bring around-the-clock operational discipline to space-domain awareness, so commanders see and understand the contested environment above.

03 Manufacturing

Cleared, Mil-spec manufacturing

We build flight hardware end to end, from prototyping to production, in a cleared 32,000 square foot electronics facility and a 10,000 square foot machine shop. We manufacture to Mil-spec under a Top Secret Facility Clearance and ITAR registration.

04 Cyber & AI/ML

Zero-trust networks, AI/ML at the edge

We defend mission systems with zero-trust cybersecurity and apply AI/ML to anomaly detection and digital twins. We make defense systems both smarter and harder to break, so the network holds when it is targeted.

05 Environmental Intelligence

Environmental intelligence for the battlespace

We turn observation into intelligence commanders can act on: geospatial and remote sensing, data assimilation, and weather and space-weather forecasting that shapes how missions are planned and flown.

NASA-proven rigor

We earned our discipline on the nation's hardest science missions. We carry that same mission assurance to defense and national security, where the people who depend on these systems cannot accept anything less.
