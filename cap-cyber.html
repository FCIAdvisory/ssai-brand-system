What We Do

We make platforms smarter and secure.

We work the full span of digital defense, from the architecture that keeps adversaries out to the AI that helps operators decide faster. Each discipline is framed by the outcome it earns the mission.

01 Outcome · Networks adversaries cannot move through

Zero-trust architecture

We design and harden networks on zero-trust principles, where no user, device, or process is trusted by default. The disciplines: identity and access management, segmentation, continuous verification, and secure systems engineering.

02 Outcome · Threats seen before they land

AI/ML anomaly detection

We apply machine learning to surface the anomaly in the noise, flagging the behavior that does not belong before it becomes an incident. The disciplines: anomaly detection, model development, threat prediction, and data engineering.

03 Outcome · Decisions that hold under pressure

Autonomy & cross-agency support

We build AI for autonomy and the cross-agency workflows that connect missions, so operators act on one trusted picture. The disciplines: autonomy engineering, decision support, secure data sharing, and mission integration.

04 Outcome · A platform you can test before you fly it

Digital twins

We build custom AI tools that stand up digital twins, high-fidelity virtual models operators use to rehearse, test, and predict. The disciplines: simulation, modeling, software engineering, and data-driven analysis.

05 Outcome · Readiness measured, not assumed

Mission readiness optimization

We turn live and simulated data into a clear read on readiness, optimizing how systems perform when the mission cannot fail. The disciplines: performance modeling, analytics, scenario simulation, and operations support.

The principle

Secure by design, sharper by data

We do not bolt security on after the fact. We build it into the architecture, then layer AI on top so the same systems that stay protected also help operators decide faster. Built for national security, focused on operational dominance.

Proven

CREST and the framework behind the twin.

Our digital-defense work is anchored by real frameworks and disciplines, not slideware. CREST is the proof of how we turn Earth and mission data into systems operators can act on.

Digital-twin framework

CREST: Coupled Reusable Earth System Tensor

CREST is an AI-enabled, Python-based digital-twin framework. It gives engineers a reusable foundation for coupling Earth-system and mission data into high-fidelity virtual models, so platforms can be simulated, tested, and optimized before and during the mission.

  • AI-enabled. Machine learning is built into the framework, not added beside it.
  • Python-based. Reusable, extensible, and ready to couple with mission data pipelines.
  • Digital twin at the core. Virtual models operators use to rehearse, test, and predict.
  • Coupled and reusable. One framework that carries across missions and agencies.
