About SSAI

We turn science into mission advantage.

Science Systems and Applications, Inc. (SSAI) delivers mission-critical science, engineering, and technology to the agencies that explore, observe, and defend. We were founded in 1977.

For more than four decades, we have turned complex science into systems that perform when the mission cannot fail. We engineer instruments and operate missions in Earth and space data, AI/ML analytics, instrument engineering, and secure IT, work trusted on NASA's flagship missions and now relied on across defense and national security.

We are a certified woman-owned, family-led small business, headquartered in the Aerospace Building minutes from NASA Goddard in the Washington D.C. defense corridor. We bring together the scientists, engineers, data analysts, and IT professionals who serve those who explore, observe, and defend, united by one commitment: to turn science into mission advantage for every customer we serve.

What We Do

Five capabilities, six markets.

We deliver mission-critical science and technology across every mission space. Five capabilities, fielded across six markets, turning science into mission advantage from NASA's flagship programs to defense and national security.

01 Capability

Science, Technology & Engineering Services

We engineer the systems the mission depends on: systems engineering, avionics and software (CMMI Level 3), FPGA design, EEE parts processing, and integration & testing. Proven on NASA flagships, now trusted across defense.

02 Capability

Space Operations

We keep missions running from pre-launch through on-orbit: deep-space instrument development and calibration, 24/7 operational modeling for NOAA and NASA, and mission operations the science cannot do without.

03 Capability

Satellite & Space Systems Manufacturing

We build flight hardware end-to-end, from prototyping to production, in a 32,000 sq ft electronics facility and a 10,000 sq ft machine shop under two miles from NASA Goddard.

04 Capability

Environmental Intelligence

We turn raw observation into intelligence the mission can act on: geospatial and remote sensing, data assimilation, oceanography, hydrology, and weather and space-weather forecasting.

05 Capability

Cybersecurity & Digital Defense

We make systems smarter and secure: advanced IT, zero-trust cybersecurity, and AI/ML for digital twins and anomaly detection, including the CREST digital-twin framework. Built to defend the mission.

Six markets

Every mission space

We serve Defense & National Security, Federal Civilian, the Intelligence Community, State & Local, Commercial, and OCONUS / International.

Three Domains, One Horizon

The Sun, the Earth, and the universe.

SSAI helps the world's science agencies see across the full reach of observation, from the star that drives our weather, to the planet we are sworn to protect, to the deep universe beyond. Three domains of science, pursued with one purpose: to turn the view from space into understanding on the ground.

01 Heliophysics

The Sun

We help observe the star at the center of it all: its flares, its storms, and the space weather that ripples outward to touch satellites, power grids, and life on Earth.

02 Earth Science · Our largest

The Earth

Our largest domain. Climate and oceans, ice and atmosphere: SSAI engineers the instruments, operates the missions, and assimilates the data that let agencies measure a changing planet and act on what they find.

03 Planetary & Astrophysics

The Universe

From Lucy and JWST to Roman, Chandra, and XRISM, we help turn light from distant worlds and ancient galaxies into the data that rewrites what we know about the cosmos.

Our History

A passion for science.

A true passion for science led to SSAI's founding as a one-person company. From that humble beginning, SSAI has grown organically into the organization it is today.

Founders Om and Sara Bahethi worked over four decades to create and maintain a culture that supports a spirit of scientific exploration and innovation, respect for our planet and its environment, and a desire to make things better for humankind.

In 2021, Chairman Om and Secretary Saraswati Bahethi passed leadership to their son, Praveen. The family-centered values that were so important at our beginning remain central to who we are today.

In Memory

The legacy of Dr. Om P. Bahethi.

For nearly 48 years, Dr. Om P. Bahethi built SSAI into a beacon of excellence in Earth and space science.
