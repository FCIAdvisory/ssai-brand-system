01 · Science, Technology & Engineering Services

Engineering excellence, flight-proven.

SSAI carries spaceflight hardware and software from requirements through verification, with the workmanship standards and quality systems that flagship missions demand.

Trusted on OCI/PACE, Roman Space Telescope, Lucy, Mars Sample Return and the Capture, Containment and Return System, Dragonfly, GeoXO, DAVINCI+, TSIS-2, and JWST. The same discipline now enables defense and national security programs.

  • Systems engineering with requirements definition, verification and validation
  • Avionics & FPGA design and software engineering at CMMI Level 3
  • EEE parts processing and J-STD-001F certified workmanship
  • System integration & testing and manufacturing engineering
  • AS9100 & ITAR quality and compliance across every build
NASA / Goddard Space Flight Center
02 · Space Operations

Deep space and space weather.

SSAI operates the models and instruments that keep national missions running, from millions of daily observations to spacecraft on orbit.

We operate NASA's GEOS Data Assimilation System, ingesting millions of observations daily, and deliver 24/7 operational modeling for NOAA and NASA. In deep space, we develop and calibrate instruments, design trajectories, and model atmospheric entry from pre-launch through on-orbit operations.

Modeling

Operational assimilation

Global atmospheric reanalysis and next-generation coupled models running around the clock.

Deep space

Instruments & operations

Instrument development and calibration, trajectory design, atmospheric-entry modeling, and data processing, archiving and distribution.

NASA / Goddard Space Flight Center

04 · Environmental Intelligence

Raw data into actionable intelligence.

SSAI transforms observations from space, air and ground into intelligence that informs decisions across the geospatial and remote-sensing domain.

Our work spans data assimilation, remote sensing, oceanography, hydrology, biosphere modeling, and weather and space-weather forecasting, drawing on decades of instrument expertise.

  • Atmosphere & composition: OMI, OMPS, TROPOMI, GEMS, EPIC
  • Land & ocean imaging: MODIS, VIIRS, MISR, AVIRIS-NG
  • Lidar & altimetry: CATS-ISS, CPL, ICESat-2
NASA / Goddard Space Flight Center

Built for operational dominance.
