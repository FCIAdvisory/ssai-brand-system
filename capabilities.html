Capabilities

Seven things you can task us to do.

Each line is a discrete, procurable capability with people, process, and flight heritage behind it — and a program a contracting officer can check. Together they cover the full mission chain: build the instrument, fly the mission, run the data system, and stand behind the science.

01 Instrument Engineering & I&T

Engineer, integrate & test flight instruments

Staff and lead instrument integration and test: I&T managers, instrument systems engineers, parts engineering, and subsystem leads for power, comms, and C&DH. SSAI is the ESES organization at Goddard — 180+ engineers building and testing spaceflight hardware.

Proof — ESES at NASA Goddard ; OCI on PACE (SSAI-led electrical engineering); Roman Wide Field Instrument.

02 Spacecraft & Mission Operations

Operate missions, payloads & instruments

Run payload and instrument operations and provide 24/7 mission-operations support — commanding, monitoring, and sustaining instruments and observatories once they are on orbit.

Proof — GOES-R series integration & test plus ongoing GOES operations; SAGE III payload operations center.

03 Ground Data Systems & DAACs

Run NASA ground systems & archives

Build and operate ground data systems, data pipelines, and NASA Distributed Active Archive Centers — the systems that ingest the downlink and turn it into archived, discoverable science data.

Proof — TEMPO data pipeline at the ASDC DAAC, NASA Langley; GSFC data systems.

04 Calibration & Validation

Calibrate & validate the measurement

Establish and maintain calibration and validation for spaceborne instruments and their data products — the rigor that makes a measurement trustworthy enough to base decisions and climate records on.

Proof — OMI / OMPS ozone calibration & validation; long-baseline cal/val across Earth-observing sensors.

05 Data Assimilation, Modeling & HPC

Assimilate data & model the Earth system

Develop and run data assimilation, Earth-system modeling, and reanalysis on high-performance computing — fusing observations into the continuous, physically consistent records agencies use to study a changing planet.

Proof — MERRA-2 reanalysis (2019 NASA Agency Honor Award); GEOS modeling.

06 Data Science & AI / ML

Apply AI/ML to science data

Build machine-learning retrievals and data-science workflows for science data at scale, and provide standards leadership across NASA's Earth Science Data System Working Groups (ESDSWG).

Proof — ML retrievals for Earth-observing data; ESDSWG leadership.

07 Scientific Research Support

Support the science itself

Provide research support across Earth science, heliophysics, and planetary & astrophysics — the scientists and analysts who define the requirements, interpret the data, and publish the results behind the missions.

Proof — Earth science, heliophysics, and planetary & astrophysics research across NASA centers.
