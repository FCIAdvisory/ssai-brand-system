The Engineering Practice

From concept to orbit.

We engineer and build the hardware and systems that fly. SSAI carries a mission from instrument and systems engineering, through flight-hardware manufacturing, precision machining, and additive fabrication, into mission operations, and across every stage of test and evaluation.

For more than four decades we have delivered on NASA's flagship programs, and that same discipline now serves defense and national security. We hold the systems engineering, avionics, FPGA, and EEE parts expertise that mission-critical hardware demands, and we back it with manufacturing and test capability under our own roof, minutes from NASA Goddard.

The result is a single accountable partner for the full build. We reduce hand-offs, we own the interfaces, and we deliver hardware that performs the first time, and every time.
