Defense Capabilities

Engineered for the missions that cannot fail.

We serve the warfighters, operators, and analysts who defend the nation, with a cleared, vertically integrated base spanning digital defense, cleared manufacturing, space operations, and mission-grade engineering.

01

Cybersecurity & Digital Defense

We make platforms smarter and secure, so operators stay ahead of the threat. Advanced IT and cybersecurity on zero-trust architectures, with AI/ML for anomaly detection, autonomy, and cross-agency support. Our CREST framework (Coupled Reusable Earth System Tensor) is an AI-enabled Python framework for operational digital twins, taking the mission from sensor to decision.

02

Satellite & Space Systems Manufacturing

We build the cleared, flight-grade hardware the mission depends on. A 32,000 sq ft cleared electronics manufacturing facility (PCB design, fab, and test; FPGA board repair; ESD-protected floor; three clean rooms; cleared personnel) and a 10,000 sq ft Mil-spec machine shop (Mil-I-45208A, Mil-std-45662; CNC, lathes, presses, mills; certified aerospace machinists) under two miles from NASA Goddard.

03

Space Operations

We keep operators ahead in a contested domain. 24/7 operational modeling, deep-space instrument development and calibration, trajectory design and atmospheric-entry modeling, and mission operations from pre-launch through on-orbit, space expertise that transfers directly to defense and resilient space.

04

Mission-Grade Engineering

We engineer the hardware that has to work the first time, and every time. Systems engineering, requirements and V&V, avionics and FPGA design, software engineering (CMMI L3), EEE parts processing, and system integration and testing, to AS9100, ITAR registration, and J-STD-001F workmanship, the rigor that flies NASA flagships, applied to defense.

05

Environmental Intelligence

We turn what sensors see into decision advantage for the operator. Data assimilation, remote sensing, weather and space-weather forecasting, and multi-source fusion for ISR and operational decisions when the mission cannot wait.
