Test, Evaluation & Integration

We retire risk where it is cheapest to retire.

A program does not get a second chance at the moment of truth. We build the evidence that a system will perform the first time, and every time, by testing it against the mission long before the mission arrives.

Delivered with our subsidiary Elucidation Concepts, SSAI provides systems integration testing, development test and evaluation, independent verification and validation, and operational test and evaluation. We bring the discipline of agile delivery and the rigor of formal evaluation to the same program, so risk is found early, decisions are backed by data, and readiness is proven rather than assumed.

We work the full lifecycle. We define what good looks like in measurable terms, instrument the system to prove it, and hand the program office an honest, traceable record of how the system behaves under load, under stress, and under the conditions the operator will actually meet.

What We Do

Five disciplines, one verdict on readiness.

We cover the test continuum end to end, from the first integration of subsystems to the operational assessment that tells a decision-maker whether the system is ready. Each discipline produces evidence the program can stand behind.

01 Integration

Systems Integration Testing

We bring subsystems together and prove they work as one. We verify interfaces, data flows, and end-to-end threads, surfacing integration faults early, where they cost a fix and not a mission.

02 DT&E

Development Test & Evaluation

We test against requirements throughout development. We design the test cases, instrument the system, and measure performance against the specification so engineering decisions rest on data rather than expectation.

03 IV&V

Independent Verification & Validation

We provide an independent line of evidence. We verify that the system was built right and validate that the right system was built, giving the program office an objective assessment free of the developer's assumptions.

04 OT&E

Operational Test & Evaluation

We assess the system the way the operator will use it. Through IOT&E and operational assessment, we evaluate effectiveness, suitability, and survivability under realistic conditions, so fielding decisions are made on operational truth.

05 Automation

Test Automation & Agile Methods

We make testing keep pace with delivery. We build automated regression suites and embed test in agile cadences, so every increment is exercised continuously and quality holds as the program moves.

The outcome

Readiness, proven

The work converges on one deliverable: a clear, traceable verdict on whether the system is ready. We reduce program risk, strengthen readiness, and give leaders the evidence to commit with confidence.

How We Work

Evidence the program can stand behind.

We plan to a test strategy, not a checklist. Before a single case runs, we agree on what the system must demonstrate and how we will measure it, so every result maps to a requirement and every requirement maps to a result.

We instrument deeply, capture cleanly, and report honestly. When a system passes, the program has traceable proof. When it does not, the program has a precise, reproducible account of what failed and why, early enough to act. That is how risk gets retired before it reaches the operator.
