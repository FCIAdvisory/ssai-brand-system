Who we are

Science and technology with passion.

Founded in 1977, SSAI is a family-led, woman-owned small business that has spent nearly 48 years alongside NASA, supporting more than 150 Earth and space science missions and turning raw observation into knowledge the world can use. Quiet, exacting, enduring: the work behind the discoveries.

Three domains, one horizon

We follow science across three reaches.

The Sun and its weather, the planets and the wider Universe, and Earth itself. Earth science is the heart of what we do, the climate, oceans, ice, and atmosphere of the world that holds life.

Earth is the heart of it

We turn instruments on the planet that needs us most.

Tracking climate, oceans, ice, and atmosphere through landmark missions. MERRA-2, our reanalysis of Earth's modern atmosphere, earned a 2019 NASA Agency Honor Award. This is not science for its own sake. It is science for life on Earth.
