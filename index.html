What we do

Turning science into mission advantage.

SSAI is a science and engineering partner to U.S. agencies, with expertise in Earth and space data, AI/ML analytics, instrument engineering, secure IT, and advanced manufacturing. For more than four decades we have turned complex science into the engineering, operations, and intelligence that national missions depend on, from NASA and NOAA to defense and national security.

01

Science, Technology & Engineering

Systems engineering, avionics, FPGA design, EEE parts, and integration and test, proven on NASA flagships and now enabling defense.

02

Space Operations

Deep space and space weather. We operate NASA's GEOS data assimilation system and provide 24/7 modeling for NASA and NOAA.

03

Satellite & Space Systems Manufacturing

A cleared electronics facility and a Mil-spec machine shop near Goddard, from rapid prototyping to flight production.

04

Environmental Intelligence

Geospatial and remote sensing that transforms raw observations into actionable intelligence for agencies and planners.

05

Cybersecurity & Digital Defense

Zero-trust architectures, AI/ML, and the CREST digital-twin framework. Built for national security.

Who we are

Science and technology with passion.

Founded in 1977, SSAI is a family-led, woman-owned small business that has spent nearly 48 years alongside NASA, supporting more than 150 Earth and space science missions and turning raw observation into knowledge the world can use. Quiet, exacting, enduring: the work behind the discoveries.

Earth is the heart of it

We turn instruments on the planet that needs us most.

Tracking climate, oceans, ice, and atmosphere through landmark missions. MERRA-2, our reanalysis of Earth's modern atmosphere, earned a 2019 NASA Agency Honor Award. This is not science for its own sake. It is science for life on Earth.
