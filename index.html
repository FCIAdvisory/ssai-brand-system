The vision

From observation to understanding.

SSAI is the science that turns what we see from space into knowledge humanity can trust — and act on. Not just the instruments and the data, but the layer that makes them mean something.

The understanding layer

We build the models, calibration, data assimilation and AI that turn raw observation into validated scientific truth.

Mission-proven heritage

Nearly five decades and 150+ missions alongside NASA and NOAA — the science partner the agencies stake decisions on.

Science for life on Earth

Cleaner air, safer skies, resilient harvests, thousands of lives saved — the human outcomes of understanding made real.
