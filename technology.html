The Technology Practice

From mission data to decision advantage.

We make the mission smarter and secure. Across AI and machine learning, cybersecurity, data science, and secure IT and computing, we turn raw mission data into intelligence the mission can act on, and we defend it at every layer.

Our work is grounded in decades of operating the data systems behind NASA and NOAA missions, where the answer has to be right and the system has to stay up. Proprietary Frameworks

How we move fast without trading away assurance.

Two frameworks let us deliver mission AI and secure systems with the rigor federal missions demand. One governs how we build and field AI. The other governs how we defend it.

IntelliCore · AI/ML Framework

From insight to operation, on a single path

IntelliCore is our proprietary AI and machine learning framework. It carries a capability through six disciplined functions so AI reaches operations as a trusted asset, not a prototype.

Discovery & Insight. Core Architecture & Model Design. Solution Development & Customization. Validation & Pilot Operations. Full-Scale Deployment & Adoption. Continuous Improvement. Each function has clear acceptance gates, so the mission knows what it is getting before it depends on it.

Inside IntelliCore
CYBERSAFE · Cyber Framework

Public-sector security, held to standard

CYBERSAFE is our cybersecurity framework for public-sector missions. It builds defense in depth on the standards federal systems are measured against, so security is provable, not asserted.

NIST Risk Management Framework. Zero Trust architecture. FedRAMP and FISMA compliance. A 24/7 Security Operations Center with continuous monitoring and rapid response. The result is a system that stays defended when the mission cannot fail.
