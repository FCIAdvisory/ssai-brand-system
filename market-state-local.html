State & Local

We bring mission-grade science and engineering to the work communities depend on.

States, counties, cities, airports, and school districts run the systems people use every day. We apply the same discipline trusted on national missions to those systems, so the infrastructure that moves, teaches, and protects communities performs the first time, and every time.

Who We Serve

Civic systems, held to a mission standard.

We serve the agencies and authorities that keep daily life running: state departments and county governments, municipal utilities, transit and aviation authorities, public safety operations, and the school systems that educate the next generation.

These missions look different from a flagship launch, but the demands are the same. The data has to be right. The systems have to stay up. The decisions have to hold under scrutiny, on a public budget and a public timeline. We engineer instruments and operate missions where failure is not an option, and we bring that same rigor to airports, classrooms, and the infrastructure underneath a region.

We treat a public-sector program with the discipline we bring to a national mission. The customer changes. The standard does not.

How We Serve State & Local

Mission discipline, applied to civic problems.

We map the capabilities trusted on national missions to the problems states, counties, cities, and districts actually solve. Each one is measured by the outcome it produces on the ground.

01 Science, Technology & Engineering

Infrastructure that performs under load

We bring systems engineering, software, and integration and test discipline to the assets a region runs on. We engineer and verify the systems behind airports, transit, and public facilities so they hold up to the demand placed on them, and so the agency can prove they do.

02 Environmental Intelligence

Decisions grounded in observation

We turn geospatial, remote sensing, weather, and hydrology data into intelligence a jurisdiction can act on. Where to harden against flood and fire, how to plan for water and air quality, and how a region responds when the environment turns.

03 Cybersecurity & Digital Defense

Public systems, defended

We secure the systems that hold a community's data and keep its services running, with zero-trust architecture, AI/ML for anomaly detection, and advanced IT. The networks behind schools, utilities, and public safety stay available and stay protected.

04 Science, Technology & Engineering

Data the public can trust

We build the data and analytics pipelines that turn raw measurement into reporting a jurisdiction can stand behind. Clean, traceable, and defensible, so leaders decide on evidence and answer to the public with confidence.

05 Cybersecurity & Digital Defense

Modernization without disruption

We modernize aging public IT in place, on the timelines and budgets the public sector lives by. Services stay online while the systems behind them are brought current, hardened, and made ready for what comes next.
