The idea

From observation to understanding.

Every direction is built on one positioning: SSAI is the layer that turns what we observe from space into knowledge humanity can trust and act on. Same logo, same palette, same type — three different ways to say it out loud.

The understanding layer

Three directions

Three ways to wear the brand.

One system, three personalities. Each uses the same building blocks and differs in attitude — data, emotion, or authority. Pick the one that sounds most like SSAI, and we build the rest of the brand around it.
