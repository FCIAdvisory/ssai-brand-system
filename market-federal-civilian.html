Federal Civilian

We build and operate the systems federal civilian science runs on.

SSAI delivers the science, engineering, data systems, and operations that civilian agencies depend on to observe the planet and act on what they see. For more than four decades we have engineered instruments, operated missions, and turned raw observation into decisions for the agencies that watch our weather, map our land, and protect our environment.

The Market

Civilian agencies carry the nation's science mission.

Federal civilian agencies observe the Earth, model its systems, and deliver the data the country relies on every day. We serve the scientists, engineers, and mission managers behind that work at NASA, NOAA, USGS, EPA, USDA, GSA, the Department of Commerce, and the Department of the Interior.

This mission cannot fail. A weather forecast, a flood warning, a climate record, a land survey: each one depends on instruments that perform on orbit, missions that run around the clock, and data that holds up under scrutiny. We engineer for that standard, the first time, and every time.

SSAI was founded in 1977 to serve this work, and it remains the heart of what we do. Our 350+ Scientists and engineers have supported more than 160 missions across 49+ years, built on the science and operations heritage that civilian agencies trust.

How We Serve Federal Civilian

From instrument to decision.

We bring the full span of our capabilities to the civilian science mission: the science and engineering that build the instruments, the operations that keep missions running, and the data systems that turn observation into the products agencies act on.

01 Science, Technology & Engineering

Instruments that perform on orbit

We engineer the systems civilian science depends on: systems engineering, avionics and software at CMMI Level 3, FPGA design, EEE parts processing, and integration and testing. Proven on NASA flagship missions, delivered to the standard the science requires.

02 Space Operations

Missions that run around the clock

We keep civilian missions running from pre-launch through on-orbit: deep-space instrument development and calibration, 24/7 operational modeling for NOAA and NASA, and the mission operations the science cannot do without.

03 Environmental Intelligence

Observation turned into decisions

We turn raw observation into intelligence agencies can act on: geospatial and remote sensing, data assimilation, oceanography, hydrology, and weather and space-weather forecasting. The work that becomes a forecast, a warning, or a record of record.

04 Satellite & Space Systems Manufacturing

Flight hardware, built to standard

We build flight hardware end-to-end, from prototyping to production, in a 32,000 sq ft electronics facility and a 10,000 sq ft machine shop under two miles from NASA Goddard. AS9100 quality, close to the missions it serves.

05 Cybersecurity & Digital Defense

Data systems that hold

We make civilian science systems smarter and secure: advanced IT, zero-trust cybersecurity, and AI/ML for digital twins and anomaly detection. The data products agencies publish are only as good as the systems that protect them.
