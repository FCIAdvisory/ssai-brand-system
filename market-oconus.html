OCONUS & International

We deliver the view that crosses borders.

Storms, oceans, ice, and air do not stop at a border, and neither does the mission. SSAI delivers Earth-observation and environmental intelligence that supports U.S. operations abroad and the international institutions that depend on a clear, shared view of a changing planet.

The Market

One planet, observed across every border.

We serve the missions that operate beyond U.S. shores: combatant commands and forces forward-deployed across theaters, federal agencies working abroad, and the multinational institutions that turn Earth observation into decisions for regions far from Washington.

OCONUS work begins with a hard truth. The phenomena that drive these missions, weather and space weather, drought and flood, sea ice and ocean state, do not recognize jurisdiction. A typhoon forming over the western Pacific, a dust storm crossing the Sahel, a phytoplankton bloom shifting a fishery: each is a global signal that has to become a local decision, often where ground data is thin and the stakes are high.

SSAI was built for that view. For more than four decades we have engineered the instruments, operated the missions, and assimilated the global data that let agencies measure the whole planet and act on what they find. We bring the same discipline to a partner nation, a forward command, or a development bank that we bring to a NASA flagship: the science holds the first time, and every time.

How We Serve

How we serve OCONUS & International.

Global reach is not a slogan. It is a set of capabilities that move observation into decision across any region on Earth, whatever the data conditions on the ground.

01 Environmental Intelligence

A clear picture, anywhere on Earth

We turn global observation into intelligence a command or institution can act on: geospatial and remote sensing, oceanography, hydrology, and weather and space-weather forecasting. Where local ground data is sparse, the view from orbit fills the gap and the decision still gets made.

02 Space Operations

Forecasts that hold across theaters

We run operational modeling around the clock for NOAA and NASA, the same discipline that keeps a forecast trustworthy over open ocean and distant coastlines. Space weather, storm tracks, and environmental conditions reach the people who plan operations far from home, on time.

03 Science, Technology & Engineering

Instruments that see the whole planet

We engineer the sensors and systems that observe Earth at global scale, proven on NASA flagships. The instruments that map a coastline, a crop belt, or an ice sheet do not change when the region does. The science crosses the border with them.

04 Environmental Intelligence

Shared data for shared problems

We assimilate observation from many sources into a single, consistent record, the kind of global reanalysis that lets U.S. agencies and international partners reason from the same picture of drought, flood, air quality, and ocean state, rather than arguing over whose data is right.

05 Cybersecurity & Digital Defense

Trusted systems, far from home

We make the systems behind the mission secure and resilient: zero-trust cybersecurity and AI/ML for anomaly detection. The data that travels across borders and networks reaches its destination protected, so the people who depend on it can trust what they see.

Proof

Trusted where the view has to travel.

Our reach is not theoretical. It is recorded in the customers we serve, the data products the world relies on, and the missions whose value crosses every border.

Customer

The World Bank

SSAI supports the World Bank, bringing NASA-grade Earth-observation and environmental analysis to an institution whose work spans developing regions across the globe, where reliable ground data is often the hardest thing to come by.

Program

GLOBE, in 125 countries

Through the GLOBE Program, SSAI supports Earth-science observation and education reaching 125 countries, 35,000+ schools, and more than 200 million measurements, a global network turning observation into shared understanding.

Data Product

Black Marble night lights

SSAI helps deliver Black Marble, the VIIRS night-lights record used worldwide to track power, population, and recovery after disaster, anywhere on Earth, from one consistent global dataset.

Data Product

MERRA-2 global reanalysis

SSAI helped deliver MERRA-2, the modern global atmospheric reanalysis, recognized with a 2019 NASA Agency Honor Award. It gives partners worldwide a single, consistent picture of the atmosphere over every region.

Capability

SARSAT search and rescue

SSAI supports the NOAA SARSAT control center, part of the international satellite system that has aided some 7,000 lives over 25+ years, an everyday demonstration that environmental systems save people across borders.

Credentials

Cleared and compliant

ISO 9001:2015 · AS9100 · CMMI Level 3 · Top Secret Facility Clearance · ITAR Registered: the standing required to support sensitive missions, at home and abroad.
