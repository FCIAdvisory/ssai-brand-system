Company · Leadership

Led by science, run with discipline.

SSAI is led by a team that pairs deep science with operational rigor, a family-led, woman-owned company carrying forward the founders' conviction that science pursued with integrity makes the world better. Founded in 1977 by Dr. Om P. and Sara Bahethi; led today by the next generation and a bench of mission veterans.

About the company Our history
The executive team

A leadership bench built where science meets the mission.

For nearly fifty years SSAI has been the preferred partner of the agencies that ask the hardest questions about our planet and the universe. That trust is earned at the bench and in the control room, and it is stewarded at the top.

In 2021, founders Chairman Om and Secretary Saraswati Bahethi passed leadership to the next generation. The people who lead SSAI today carry a rare combination: a family's nearly five-decade commitment to scientific exploration, the financial and operational discipline of a serious enterprise, and the lived experience of federal science and national mission operations. The family-centered values that were central at our founding remain central to who we are.

Below, the five executives who set the course, what they lead, and the experience they bring to it.

Executive officers

Dr. Shilpa Bahethi, MD

A member of the Bahethi family and the company's former corporate treasurer, Dr. Bahethi leads SSAI as Chief Executive Officer. Chief Operating Officer

Kevin Cooley

SSAI's Chief Operating Officer joined from the most senior ranks of federal science. Kevin Cooley served as Environmental Intelligence Advisor to the U.S. Department of Homeland Security and as the NOAA–DHS liaison, and directed the National Weather Service Office of Planning and Programming, leading 1,500 personnel and $650M in annual mission capabilities. The line of succession

From one founder's passion to a generational stewardship.

A true passion for science led to SSAI's founding in 1977 as a one-person company. From that humble beginning it grew organically into the organization it is today.

Founders Om and Sara Bahethi worked over four decades to create and maintain a culture that supports a spirit of scientific exploration, respect for our planet and its environment, and a desire to make things better for humankind. For nearly 48 years, Dr. Om P. Bahethi built SSAI into a beacon of excellence in Earth and space science, his work with NASA and NOAA embodying a lifelong passion for discovery. That passion endures beyond the company, through the Bahethi Family Foundation, scholarships, and a commitment to STEM equity. In 2021, leadership passed to their son, Praveen, and the values that were so important at our beginning remain central to who we are today.

NASA / Goddard Space Flight Center / Bill Hrybyk
One standard, every domain

Leadership in service of life on Earth.

The view from space is only the beginning. What our leaders steward is the long, patient work of turning that view into understanding on the ground, across the Sun, the Earth, and the universe beyond.

From a sensor in a clean room to a data product on a scientist's screen, the standard set at the top holds across every mission SSAI touches: trust, scientific integrity, and accountability for everything we do.

How they lead

Five values held at the executive level.

SSAI's leadership does not treat values as decoration. They are the operating standard, held across every mission the company touches, and the reason agencies have trusted SSAI for nearly five decades.

  • Trust, we instill trust through honesty and transparency.
  • Integrity, we exercise scientific integrity and uncompromising engineering as the foundation of our professional ethics.
  • Teamwork, we build strong teams to execute our business practices.
  • Passion, we innovate and evolve by engaging our collective sense of wonder and enthusiasm.
  • Accountability, we take ownership of our decisions and everything we do.
