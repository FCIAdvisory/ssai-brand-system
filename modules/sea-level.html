Loads the real Nile Delta scene and paints the true-color basemap immediately on init (no interaction or scroll gating). Drag the sea-level slider from 0 to +2.0 m, or tap a scenario preset (Today, 2050 Low/High, 2100 Low/High, +2.0 m), to raise the water: a translucent ocean-blue overlay floods inward from the existing coast and channels, deepening in color over the lowest ground, with an accent-colored waterline that shimmers along the advancing shoreline. The HUD updates live — sea-level rise in metres, estimated land inundated %, and the year/emissions scenario — and the matching preset and on-image scenario chip highlight. Animation (waterline shimmer) pauses when the module scrolls off-screen and is disabled entirely under prefers-reduced-motion (still fully interactive, repainting on each slider change). The accent color is read live from --accent and applied to the slider, eyebrow, chip, waterline and main stat. Data · NASA MODIS — Nile Delta
