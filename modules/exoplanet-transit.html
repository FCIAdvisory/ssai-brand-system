Click any planet tab to load its real parameters — the star disk resizes and recolors, the orbiting planet rescales, and the light curve below redraws with that planet's true transit depth and period. The simulation runs continuously: watch the planet sweep across the stellar disk while a tracing dot rides the live flux curve and dips exactly when the planet occults the star. A live readout shows period, transit depth, planet radius, and current normalized flux. Play/Pause toggles the animation. Respects reduced-motion (renders a static representative frame) and pauses when scrolled off-screen. Data · NASA Exoplanet Archive (Kepler/TESS)
