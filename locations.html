Headquarters

The Aerospace Building, Lanham, Maryland

Since our founding, SSAI has sat where the science is decided. Our headquarters occupies The Aerospace Building, directly adjacent to NASA Goddard Space Flight Center — close enough that our scientists and engineers move between our offices and the center's clean rooms, control rooms, and data systems as a single working community.

It is a deliberate kind of proximity. For nearly fifty years, being beside Goddard has meant being beside the missions themselves — from instrument integration to mission operations to the data archives that turn observations into understanding.

Our people work where the science is

A headquarters address tells only part of the story. SSAI's strength is presence — scientists, engineers, data specialists, and operators embedded directly alongside the agencies they support, on the centers and campuses where missions are built, flown, and analyzed.

That on-site model is how a small business reaches across more than 150 Earth and space science missions: by standing next to the instrument in the clean room, next to the controller at the console, and next to the archive that serves the data to the world.

Presence, not just address

SSAI holds CMMI Level 3 and ISO 9001:2015 certifications and is a certified SBA small business — the disciplines that let a single, focused company maintain trusted, on-site relationships across this many partners at once.

Whether the work is at our Lanham headquarters or at a partner's facility a continent away, the standard is the same: science and engineering held to integrity, delivered next to the people who depend on it.
