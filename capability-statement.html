Core Competencies

What we do.

Seven procurable service lines, delivered on-site across the NASA, NOAA, and federal science enterprise.

  • Instrument Engineering, I&TThe ESES organization at NASA Goddard — instrument systems engineering, integration & test, parts engineering, and power / comms / C&DH subsystem leadership.
  • Spacecraft & Mission OperationsPayload and instrument operations with 24/7 mission-operations support across active flight programs.
  • Ground Data Systems & DAACsNASA Distributed Active Archive Centers, data pipelines, and long-term science archives.
  • Calibration & ValidationSensor calibration and on-orbit data validation that keeps science measurements trustworthy.
  • Data Assimilation, Modeling & HPCMERRA-2 and GEOS reanalysis and modeling on high-performance computing systems.
  • Data Science & AI/MLMachine-learning retrievals and Earth Science Data Systems Working Group (ESDSWG) leadership.
  • Scientific Research SupportEarth science, heliophysics, planetary science, and astrophysics research across the agency mission set.
Differentiators

Why SSAI.

  • Beside NASA Goddard. Headquartered in the Aerospace Building adjacent to Goddard Space Flight Center — embedded where the missions are built and flown.
  • ~48 years of heritage. Founded 1977; ~750 employees supporting 175+ active contracts across 150+ missions.
  • 180+ ESES engineers. A standing instrument engineering and integration-and-test workforce at Goddard.
  • Certified WOSB. SBA-certified woman-owned small business (2024) — small under NAICS 541715.
  • CMMI Level 3 for Development & ISO 9001:2015. Appraised process maturity paired with a certified quality management system.
  • Leadership pedigree. Chief Operating Officer is a former National Weather Service Senior Executive Service leader; Chief Corporate Scientist brings 25 years as a NASA Goddard scientist.

Proof of Mission

Where our work flies.

  • MERRA-2 — 2019 NASA Agency Honor Award for atmospheric reanalysis.
  • GOES-R series — integration & test plus ongoing operations at NOAA's Satellite Operations Facility.
  • TEMPO & SAGE III — data pipeline at the ASDC DAAC and payload operations center at NASA Langley.
  • NOAA SARSAT — search-and-rescue supporting ~7,000 lives saved over 25+ years.
  • OCI / PACE, TIRS-2 / Landsat-9, Roman WFI — SSAI-led electrical engineering across flagship instruments.
