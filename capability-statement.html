Core Capabilities

The science and engineering behind the mission.

We serve the scientists, engineers, forecasters, analysts, and operators who explore, observe, and defend. Five capabilities carry the mission from research and instrument engineering to manufacturing, environmental intelligence, and secure digital defense.

01

Science, Technology & Engineering Services

We engineer the instruments and systems that have to work the first time, on missions that cannot fail. The disciplines behind that outcome: systems engineering, requirements and V&V, avionics design, software engineering (CMMI L3), FPGA design, EEE parts processing, system integration and testing, and manufacturing engineering, to AS9100, ITAR, and J-STD-001F workmanship.

02

Space Operations

We keep the nation watching the planet and the space around it, every day the mission demands. We operate NASA's GEOS Data Assimilation System (millions of observations daily), global atmospheric reanalysis, and 24/7 operational modeling for NOAA and NASA, plus deep-space instrument development and calibration, trajectory design, and mission operations from pre-launch through on-orbit.

03

Satellite & Space Systems Manufacturing

We build flight hardware engineered to work the first time, and every time. A 32,000 sq ft electronics manufacturing facility and a 10,000 sq ft machine shop under two miles from NASA Goddard, with an ESD-protected floor, three clean rooms, cleared personnel, Mil-spec CNC machining, and certified aerospace machinists, end to end from prototyping to production.

04

Environmental Intelligence

We turn what sensors see into decisions the mission can act on. Data assimilation, remote sensing, oceanography, hydrology, biosphere modeling, and weather and space-weather forecasting, across instruments including OMI, OMPS, MODIS, VIIRS, MISR, ICESat-2, AVIRIS-NG, TROPOMI, and GEMS.

05

Cybersecurity & Digital Defense

We make platforms smarter and secure, so the mission stays ahead. Advanced IT, cybersecurity on zero-trust architectures, and AI/ML for digital twins, anomaly detection, and cross-agency support, anchored by CREST (Coupled Reusable Earth System Tensor), an AI-enabled Python digital-twin framework. Engineered for national security, designed to work every time.
