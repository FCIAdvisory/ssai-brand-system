A program built around total well-being

Our benefits focus on total well-being, with the flexibility and choice to support your health, retirement, career goals, and work-life balance.

That means more than a list of plans. Coverage extends to employees' families and domestic partners. Time off comes on top of every federal holiday. And investing in our people sits at the center of our strategy, through SSAI University, our centralized umbrella for learning and development. The result is a place where you can stay challenged intellectually, be rewarded for excellent work, and still find room for a life outside it.

SSAI has been named a Washington Post Top Workplace five years running, and to TopWorkplaces' 2025 Professional Development list, recognition that comes from the people who actually work here.

Belonging & balance

A culture that welcomes everyone

NASA / Goddard Space Flight Center / Bill Hrybyk

SSAI's goal is to cultivate a diverse culture of creativity, collaborative innovation, and belonging, an environment where all are welcomed, heard, and celebrated in an equitable and supportive manner. A central theme of our culture is service.

Many of our people work in badgeless environments alongside government scientists and fellow contractors, with flexible hours and locations and a genuine commitment to work-life balance. Benefits are one expression of that commitment; the everyday culture is another.
