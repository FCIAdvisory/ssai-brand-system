SSAI · Modular interactive system

The Interactive Kit

A library of self-contained, reusable interactive modules in the SSAI style: a rotating dot-globe, a living Sun, a deep-field explorer, mission tools, data-as-art and more. Drop any module straight into the website or a deck. Every module is vanilla (no dependencies), responsive, and reduced-motion aware. The Earth is only one of many.
