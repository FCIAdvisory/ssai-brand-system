Mission IT & HPC

Compute at mission scale.

We develop and operate large-scale data-center systems and the high-performance computing environments that turn petabytes of observation into usable results. We do this where downtime is not an option.

For nearly fifty years we have stood up the IT and supercomputing that the nation's hardest science depends on, from secure enterprise networks to operational HPC clusters that run around the clock. Our teams have engineered and sustained the environments behind NASA's Center for Climate Simulation (NCCS) and the NASA Advanced Supercomputing (NAS) facility, processing, moving, storing, and visualizing data at a scale few organizations operate.

We bring that same discipline to defense and federal civilian missions: secure-by-design IT, high-throughput data pipelines, and computing infrastructure engineered to perform when the mission cannot fail.

What We Do

From data center to decision.

We own the full path the data travels: the facilities that house it, the systems that secure it, the compute that crunches it, and the visualization that makes it understandable to the people who act on it.

01 Data-center systems

Large-scale systems development & operations

We architect, build, and operate enterprise data-center environments end to end: compute, storage, networking, and the automation that keeps them running. We sustain operational systems around the clock, with the monitoring and resilience that mission timelines require.

02 Secure IT

Secure, mission-ready infrastructure

We engineer IT that is secure by design: hardened networks, identity and access control, and continuous monitoring aligned to federal and DoD requirements. We hold a Top Secret Facility Clearance and deliver to ISO 9001:2015 and CMMI Level 3 standards.

03 High-performance computing

HPC at mission scale

We stand up and operate high-performance computing clusters for modeling, simulation, and analysis. Our teams support the large-scale supercomputing environments behind NASA climate and Earth-system science, scheduling workloads, tuning performance, and keeping jobs running.

04 Data processing

Mission-scale data pipelines

We move, ingest, and process data at petabyte scale: high-throughput pipelines, distributed storage, and the data management that turns raw downlinks and model output into products people can use. We engineer for volume, velocity, and reliability.

05 Visualization

Scientific visualization at scale

We make massive datasets legible, from hyperwall and exploration-theater visualization to interactive analysis environments. We turn billions of data points into imagery and dashboards that let scientists and operators see what the data is telling them.

06 Cloud & hybrid

Cloud, on-premises, and hybrid

We deliver across cloud, on-premises, and hybrid architectures, matching the environment to the mission. We migrate, modernize, and operate workloads where security, cost, and performance demands point, without locking the customer in.
